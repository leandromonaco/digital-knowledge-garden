## üöÄ How to Use These Prompts

### Step 1: Choose Your AI Assistant

These prompts work with any AI assistant:
- **ChatGPT** (OpenAI)
- **Claude** (Anthropic)
- **GitHub Copilot Chat**
- **Bing Chat**
- **Google Bard**
- Any other large language model with good reasoning capabilities

### Step 2: Copy the Prompt

1. Open the appropriate review prompt document
2. Copy the entire prompt (everything in the document)
3. Paste it into your AI assistant

### Step 3: Provide Your Content

After pasting the prompt, add:
```
Here is the [note/vault data/MOC] to review:

[Paste your content here]
```

### Step 4: Review & Implement

The AI will provide:
- Structured analysis
- Specific recommendations
- Prioritized action items
- Examples and suggestions

Review the output and implement the recommendations that make sense for your use case.

## üí° Pro Tips

### Maximize Effectiveness

1. **Be Specific**: Provide context about your goals and use cases
2. **Iterate**: Use prompts multiple times as you make improvements
3. **Customize**: Adapt prompts to your specific needs and preferences
4. **Track Progress**: Keep notes on improvements over time
5. **Combine Tools**: Use prompts alongside [[GitHub Actions Guide|automated checks]]

### Example Workflow

**Weekly Maintenance Session** (30 minutes):
1. Run [[GitHub Actions Guide|automated health check]] (automated)
2. Use [[Obsidian Note Review Prompt]] on 2-3 seedling notes (10 min)
3. Fix broken links identified by automation (5 min)
4. Use [[Obsidian MOC Review Prompt]] on active MOC (10 min)
5. Update tags based on recommendations (5 min)

**Monthly Deep Review** (60 minutes):
1. Gather vault statistics (5 min)
2. Use [[Obsidian Vault Health Review Prompt]] (15 min)
3. Review and prioritize recommendations (10 min)
4. Address top 3 critical issues (20 min)
5. Plan next month's improvements (10 min)

## üÜò Troubleshooting

**Q: The AI's recommendations don't fit my workflow**
A: Customize the prompts to match your needs, or cherry-pick relevant suggestions

**Q: I'm getting generic responses**
A: Provide more context about your vault's purpose, domain, and goals

**Q: The analysis is too long**
A: Ask the AI to "provide only the top 3 recommendations" or focus on specific sections

**Q: How do I choose which prompt to use?**
A: Start with individual note reviews (easiest), then MOC reviews, then vault health (most comprehensive)

**Q: Can I use these prompts with non-Obsidian tools?**
A: Yes! The principles apply to any note-taking system, though some syntax is Obsidian-specific
